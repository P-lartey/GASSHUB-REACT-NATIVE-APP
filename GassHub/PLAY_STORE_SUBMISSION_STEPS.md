## 💡 **Pro Tips**

### Avoid Common Rejections:

**1. Crashes/Bugs**
- Test thoroughly before submission
- Fix any crashes immediately

**2. Incomplete Metadata**
- Fill ALL required fields
- Don't leave anything blank

**3. Misleading Description**
- Be accurate about what app does
- Don't overpromise

**4. Privacy Issues**
- Have valid privacy policy
- Be honest about data collection

**5. Intellectual Property**
- Use only your own assets
- Don't copy others' content

---

## 🆘 **If Rejected**

**Don't panic!** It happens often.

**Steps:**
1. Read rejection email carefully
2. Fix the specific issue(s)
3. Update app or metadata
4. Resubmit (no additional fee)
5. Usually approved within 1-2 days

**Common fixes:**
- Update description
- Add more screenshots
- Clarify privacy policy
- Fix minor bugs
